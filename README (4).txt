PROJECT OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------
This project presents a professionally designed, luxury-themed website for Tusha Petals & Gifts, a florist business based in Mbombela. The website is developed to provide a visually appealing and user-friendly platform where customers can explore floral products, learn about the business, and place orders seamlessly via WhatsApp.

The design focuses on elegance, emotional appeal, and ease of navigation, ensuring that users have a premium digital experience that reflects the brand’s identity.

--------------------------------------------------
WEBSITE GOALS AND OBJECTIVES
--------------------------------------------------
- Establish a strong online presence for the business
- Showcase floral products in a visually compelling way
- Enable quick and direct customer communication
- Increase customer engagement and conversions
- Ensure full responsiveness across devices

--------------------------------------------------
KEY FEATURES AND FUNCTIONALITY
--------------------------------------------------
- Fully responsive modern UI design
- Interactive product gallery with hover effects
- Direct WhatsApp ordering integration
- Smooth scrolling navigation
- Contact form with WhatsApp messaging function
- Testimonials section for social proof
- Floating WhatsApp quick-access button
- Structured sections (Hero, About, Shop, Why Us, Contact)
